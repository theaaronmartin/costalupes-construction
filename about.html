James Costalupes General Contractor
Design To Build

As licensed general engineering and general building contractors, we provide a full service of structural and design to build capabilities.



Costalupes Construction provides the highest grade mechanical drafting with the support of the finest structural engineers which results in construction documentation second to none. Our design team has "hands on" construction background, so we won't design an un-buildable or cost prohibited assemblies.



James Costalupes General Contractor, licensed in California and Nevada, has been building fine homes for over 30 years locally (Lake Tahoe area). The Owner, James Costalupes, has earned a reputation for superior craftsmanship, creativity, service and professionalism.



Projects are managed by a professional General Contractor and project management team, representing our customer's building project from conception to completion. Building or remodeling homes in the Lake Tahoe area, has special environmental design regulations set by the Tahoe Regional Planning Agency (TRPA) including BMP compliance, and engineering requirements, that our building team has been complying to for many years.



We are a full service construction company with in-house design team who listens to our customer's ideas and converts their architectural vision to concept. Our design and drafting specialists are Certified (green professional designer), providing Eco friendly building solutions sensitive to the Lake Tahoe and global environments.
